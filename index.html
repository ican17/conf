About the Conference

Overview

Second language writing is a demanding, multifaceted skill involving linguistic, cultural, and rhetorical dimensions. This conference brings together teachers, researchers, and doctoral students to share and discuss key issues in L2 writing and research writing, including professional development and the implications of generative AI.
